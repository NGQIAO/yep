Dim Sum

An Unusual! Refined Cuisine! A Mix Of Aromas! Sensations and Flavors Designed to Amaze YOU and Delight even the Most Difficult Plates. Each country has its own traditions, not only typical dishes but also different habits. Dimsum is the term used in southern China to indicate a meal, particularly on Sundays or on particular holidays, consisting of small portions of dishes with meat, fish or vegetables, but also sweets or fruit. Here you can try different types of delicious ravioli, from the most traditional with meat or fish, to the more particular with truffles or larded in which you can feel a strong influence of Tuscan cuisine, but the menu also hides other surprises, a wide choice of starters and second courses and above all the very particular noodles, in broth or sautéed with meat, fish or vegetable seasonings to satisfy all palates. We will welcome you with a cup of tea, which in addition to welcoming you, will allow you to try this particular combination with our dishes. Tea in the East is often used to accompany main meals, each type of tea must be served at the right temperature and with the right infusion times, and this is how you will try it at dimsum. You can then decide whether to continue your meal by choosing and sipping a tea from our selection or opting for one of our selected labels.
